How It Works

Our intelligent traffic control system uses computer vision algorithms to detect vehicle density at intersections and automatically adjusts signal timings to optimize traffic flow.

The system also incorporates hand gesture recognition for emergency manual control, allowing traffic personnel to override automated signals when necessary for special situations.
